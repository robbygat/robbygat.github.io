About

I am residing in Worcester, Massachusetts, and a recent graduate of the University of Massachusetts Amherst, where I earned a B.A. with a concentration in Computational Linguistics and Language Technology, graduating on the Dean’s List. As a linguist specializing in computational linguistics, I work in language education with agentic AI and mobile applications, and I am currently trying to incorporate PyTorch for language education deep learning experiments. I am excited to demonstrate how my strong technical background and interpersonal communication abilities make me an excellent addition to innovative teams.

At UMass Amherst, home to a world-renowned linguistics department, I conducted pioneering research on effective communication strategies for language acquisition through agentic mobile applications. My independent capstone project resulted in a Swift-based iOS application that leveraged Large Language Models (LLMs) and AI APIs to enhance language learning for children and adults. This project introduced novel technology to the field and earned commendation from international experts in linguistics and second-language pedagogy. My work showcased my ability to integrate AI applications with user-focused design to create impactful solutions.

Fluent in English and conversational in Japanese and Korean, I bring linguistic versatility and cultural adaptability to global teams. I excel at collaborating with enthusiastic students and team members, and I am committed to giving my full effort to foster enthusiasm in any work environment.

My leadership abilities have also been recognized outside of academia. I am eager to tackle new challenges, contribute innovative solutions beyond the basic job description, and grow under the guidance of experienced mentors. Thank you for considering my profile. I look forward to demonstrating how my drive and creative mindset can help advance team objectives.
