✈️ Koh Samui Travel Tips

🚗 Getting Around

  • Download the Grab app (Thailand’s version of Uber) and add your card before arrival.
  • If your villa (Airbnb) is on a hill, ask if Grab cars can access it. If not, villa staff can arrange local taxis.

📱 SIM & Connectivity

  • Best data options:
  • Airalo eSIM
  • AIS or True Move physical SIM (available at Samui airport or 7-Eleven)

💵 Money & ATMs

ATMs and exchange booths are everywhere - airport, beach road, malls, and every 7-Eleven.

🤝 Tipping Etiquette

Not mandatory but appreciated. Around 10% or rounding up fares is common for great service.

🏝️ About Samui

A small island (52 km around).

🧭 Best Activities for Wedding Guests in Koh Samui

Beaches, waterfalls, temples, shopping, spas, and beach clubs.

💧 Stay Hydrated!

Only drink bottled water (tap water isn’t drinkable). Rehydration drinks are sold in all 7-Elevens.

🦟 Mosquitoes

Some inland or garden areas can have mosquitoes, especially at sunset time. Available repellents can be found easily at 7-Eleven or local pharmacies.
